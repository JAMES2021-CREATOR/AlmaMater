Our Academic Programs

Lovable Academy offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge students academically and inspire lifelong learning.

Primary School

Foundational education focused on literacy, numeracy, and social skills for young learners.

  • English & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Nature Studies
  • Arts & Crafts

Secondary School

Intermediate education to prepare students for advanced studies and career readiness.

  • English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics & Statistics
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • ICT & Computer Science
  • Social Studies

Senior School / Advanced Level

Specialized programs aimed at preparing students for tertiary education and leadership roles.

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Economics & Business Studies
  • ICT & Programming
  • Arts & Humanities

Extra-Curricular Activities

At Lovable Academy, we believe learning extends beyond the classroom. Our students are encouraged to explore talents, develop skills, and grow socially through a variety of activities.

Sports & Fitness

Our sports program fosters teamwork, discipline, and physical wellness.

  • Football, Basketball, Volleyball
  • Swimming & Athletics
  • Martial Arts & Gymnastics
  • Inter-school competitions & tournaments

Arts & Creativity

Students explore creativity and expression through arts, music, and performing arts.

  • Painting, Sculpture & Crafts
  • Choir & Music Classes
  • Drama & Theatre Productions
  • Annual Arts & Music Festival

Clubs & Leadership

We empower students to lead, organize, and participate in clubs that match their interests.

  • Science & Robotics Club
  • Debate & Public Speaking Club
  • Environmental & Sustainability Club
  • Student Council & Leadership Programs

Community Service

Students develop empathy and social responsibility through community involvement.

  • Volunteer Work & Charity Programs
  • Environmental Clean-up Projects
  • Mentoring & Tutoring Younger Students
  • Fundraising & Awareness Campaigns

STEM & Technology

Encouraging problem-solving, innovation, and tech skills among students.

  • Robotics & Coding Workshops
  • Science Experiments & Labs
  • Tech Competitions & Hackathons
  • STEM Fairs & Exhibitions

Cultural & Social Activities

Students celebrate diversity and engage in cultural awareness activities.

  • Annual Cultural Day & Festivals
  • Language & Literature Clubs
  • International Exchange Programs
  • School Social Events & Gatherings
